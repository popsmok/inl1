Pancakes


  • Ingrediens for 4 portions
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 dl flour
  • 8 dl milk
  • A dash of salt

  1. Mix all of the ingrediens in a bowl
  2. Fry 1 dl of the batter at the time
  3. Serve pancakes with homemade strawberry jam, whipped cream and ice cream
